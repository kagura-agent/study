APPLIEDCONCEPT

📉 ai-memory M8 Retention Decay → Smarter Wiki Retirement

Replaced discrete bucket scoring in retire-candidates.sh with exponential decay: retention = e^(-λ*age) × (1 + σ*ln(1+recalls)) × recency_boost. Borrowed from ai-memory's M8 formula. Results: candidates reduced 458→367 (−20%), max score dropped from 85→65 (no more premature "strong retire" flags), and notes with active recall history are properly protected.

Takeaway: Discrete scoring (age buckets) creates artificial clustering — most notes scored 75-85 regardless of actual staleness. Continuous decay differentiates meaningfully. Notes that are recalled stay; notes that aren't fade naturally. Biology-inspired, math-verified.
